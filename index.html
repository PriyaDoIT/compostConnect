Will you be part of the solution?

Composting is an easy way to divert food waste from landfills. Together, we can make a dent in the 55,000,000 pounds of food that Chicagoans throw out every month.

Compost Connect links urban farms, community gardens, and other sites that have composting programs with local businesses and individuals who are seeking to reduce their food waste. Help build greener communities by adding your composting program to our map, or finding one near you.
