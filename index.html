Will you be part of the solution?

Composting is an easy way to divert food waste from landfills. Together, we can make a dent in the 660,000,000 pounds of food that Chicagoans throw out every year.

Compost Connect links urban farms, community gardens, and other sites that have composting programs with local businesses and individuals who are seeking to reduce their food waste. If your organization currently composts, and is willing and able to accept additional materials from local businesses and individuals who are passionate about reducing food waste, please add your site to our map. If you are a restaurant, cafe, or individual looking to start composting, browse our map to find a site near you. Or learn more about starting your own compost system!
